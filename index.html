Our Story

We Exist Because Breakups Are Hard

Breakup Delivery™ was born from a simple truth: ending relationships is one of the most emotionally charged, communication-heavy things a human being can do — and almost nobody is equipped to do it well.

We're not here to judge why you're ending things. We're here to make sure that when you do, it's handled with honesty, dignity, and clarity — for both parties involved.

Our team of writers, relationship communication specialists, and delivery professionals have helped over 2,000 people navigate one of life's most uncomfortable moments. We've seen it all — long-distance situations, situationships, serious multi-year relationships, and everything in between.
